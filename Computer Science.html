COMPUTER SCIENCE

Course Description

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science prepares students to be highly competent and certified in the areas of computing theory and application. It also teaches students to design, implement and analyze solutions to problems that arise in everyday life. This course is recommended for accelerated mathematics students and any student entering a math, business or science related field.

This course covers the theoretical and practical aspects of computing technology, including programming, data structures, algorithms, and systems design.

Computer Science is the study of computers, their systems, and how they solve problems. It focuses on understanding how computers function and how to use technology to create solutions for real-world challenges. This course covers both the theory and practical aspects of computing, including software development, hardware design, data management, and system operations. Students explore topics like algorithms, programming languages, databases, networks, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Computer Science is essential for anyone who wants to innovate, improve, or work with technology in various fields.

What to learn

Through this course, students will learn to write code in programming languages like Python, Java, or C++, solve complex problems using algorithms, and design efficient computer systems. They will also gain knowledge about managing data, securing networks, and understanding the future of technology, such as machine learning and robotics. With a foundation in Computer Science, students can pursue careers in software engineering, data analysis, cybersecurity, and more.

  • Programming Languages
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Systems Design
  • Computer Architecture
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Database Management

Average tuition per semester

The average tuition per semester for a Computer Science program varies widely, ranging from P60,000 to P 90,000 depending on the institution and country. This investment prepares students for a high-demand field with excellent career prospects.
